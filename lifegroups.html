 LIFE GROUPS
GET INVOLVED!

Life Groups will meet on Sunday afternoon or evening from January through October. Every effort is being made to keep the group size around 10 to 14 adults per group.

Below is a list of the Life Group locations as well as their leaders and their host houses and addresses. You're encouraged to participate in this rewarding ministry. Sign up by contacting Jason Poynter at 625-4110 or the church office at 439-9761. We're looking foward to a great year in Life Groups!

Leaders and Locations

Life Groups, Enterprise Boulevard's home Bible study ministry, are back! Participation in a Life Group is an excellent way to meet new people, celebrate life's good times, draw strength in life's tough times, and grow closer to the Lord and each other as we study, pray and fellowship together.
